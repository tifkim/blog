I was extremely naive to what Wordpress could do. I knew it was a powerful platform given the prevalence of websites I see leveraging this system. I'm trying to teach myself this, but even with the assistance of Treehouse Academy and other tutorials, it can be an overwhelming maze! I wish there was a 10,000ft infographic that could tell me what the impact of these functionalities are on the architecture/design of the finished product.

Maybe I was overthinking this, but I was pondering for the better part of an hour whether I truly needed a post type or could get by with categories/tags. Aiiya.

I found a few things that helped, like this WP Beginner article. My compulsive curiosity is sated to the point where I can start experimenting with these tools without being distracted by confusion, but I don't think I'll have a strong grasp until I have a running website.
