Some things to help you get through today:

  1. A common tip I come across is to get your most unpleasant task out of your way. Admittedly, I still struggle with this it makes a big difference on the days I'm able to do this.
  2. Watch Shia's intense motivational speech. If you're late to watching this like I was, I warn you that there's a lot of yelling. :)
  3. Check out my Boss Says What app for some motivational quotes (shameless self-plug): http://splurty-tifkim.herokuapp.com/
  4. Get yourself pumped with some music. I might publish a playlist on a future Monday but here are some random faves.
    • Bop around guilt-free to some TSwift "Shake It Off" (cop car version)
    • Get in the "you do you" mindset with Jessie J's "Masterpiece"
    • Catch Paul Rudd on Lip Sync Battle (especially when he sings "Don't Stop Me Now" in the second round)
  5. Check out Minion Monday for lovely memes such as the one featured. You might find an email occasion where this is appropriately and could lighten someone else's Monday. There's also a few interesting Pinterest boards with similar collections.

Cheers.
